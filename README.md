# Korean Beauty Authority

## Overview
Korean Beauty Authority is a knowledge-driven digital platform focused on Korean skincare education and product guidance.

The goal is to build:
- authority
- audience trust
- data-driven growth
- scalable digital presence

This is not a spam product page.
It is an educational brand.

---

## Vision
To become a recognized authority in Korean skincare for European and global audiences.

Education first.  
Value first.  
Products as recommendations.

---

## Philosophy
- Audience ownership
- Long-term brand equity
- Automation for scalability
- Ethical digital marketing
- Data-driven strategy

---

## Business Model
Content → Trust → Audience → Data → Conversion → Revenue

Explanation:
We build knowledge and trust.
An informed audience chooses products.
Revenue is a natural consequence.

---

## Target Markets
- Germany (primary)
- Turkey (secondary)
- Western Europe (expansion)

Each market requires tailored content and cultural understanding.
